Hello my name is James Friddle. I was born September 26, 1992 and am 30 years old. I spent most of my childhood growing up in Summerfield north Carolina and now reside in the triad area of North Carolina. I grew up in a two-parent Christian household. I have one older sister who is 33 years old. I currently am enrolled in a Community College in North Carolina and am majoring in Cyber Security. I have always found computers very interesting and loved tinkering with them. Technology has always piqued my interest and I am always wanting to learn more about the newest gadgets whenever they come out.

My Beliefs

As I said before I grew up in a Christian household. Even though being raised by Christian parents I didn't really fully accept God into my life until my freshman year in college (2012). Even though I am a Christian I still have some beliefs that "old timey" Christians my not believe in. I guess you would call this the modern day Christian. I am definitely an ally of the LGBTQ community. I have many friends who are gay or pan.

My life experience

My life experience so far has been quite an interesting one. It has taught me that I need to be careful in who to trust and who I choose to be friends with. There's definitely been a lot of ups and alot of down's in my life. However I still try to look at everything with a positive outlook. I used to tend to wear my heart on my sleeve but through my life experiences it taught me life can be hard and you need to shield and protect it.

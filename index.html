My name is James Friddle. I was born September 26, 1992 and am 30 years old. I spent most of my childhood growing up in Summerfield north Carolina and now reside in High Point, North Carolina. I grew up in a two-parent Christian household. I have one older sister who is 33 years old. I currently am enrolled at Wake Tech Community College and am majoring in Cyber Security. I have always found computers very interesting and loved tinkering with them. Technology has always piqued my interest and I am always wanting to learn more about the newest gadgets whenever they come out.
